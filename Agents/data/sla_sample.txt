### *2.0. Agreement Overview*

This section provides a high-level overview of the agreement, its purpose, and the terms used within.

#### *2.1. SLA Introduction*

This is a Service Level Agreement (SLA) between *Innovate Corp* (hereafter referred to as "Customer") and *Quantum IT Solutions* (hereafter referred to as "Service Provider"). This document identifies the Managed IT Services required and the expected level of service delivery between *November 1, 2025, to October 31, 2026*.

This agreement covers the comprehensive IT infrastructure support, network management, helpdesk services, and security monitoring as detailed herein.

Subject to review and renewal scheduled by *September 30, 2026*.

*Signatories:*

Ananya Sharma, Director of Services, Quantum IT Solutions
Rohan Gupta, CTO, Innovate Corp

* Requirements for IT services that will be provisioned to [Customer].

* Criteria for target fulfillment evaluation, including reporting and service credit policies.
* Roles and responsibilities of [Service Provider] and [Customer].

### *3.0. Service Agreement*

This section defines the specific services, performance metrics, and responsibilities.

The following table details the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and their committed service levels.

| Metric | Commitment | Measurement Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| *Helpdesk CSAT Score* | >= 4.5 / 5.0 (Average customer satisfaction) | Surveys sent to users upon ticket resolution. |

#### *3.2. Service Levels, Rankings, and Priority*

Incidents will be prioritized based on business impact, with response and resolution times assigned accordingly.

| Severity Level | Description | Target Response | Target Resolution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *1. Critical* | System-wide outage. Affects all users (e.g., primary server down, internet outage, security breach). | 15 Minutes | 4 Hours |
| *2. High* | Significant service degradation. Affects a whole department or a critical business function (e.g., ERP application slow, department printer offline). | 30 Minutes | 8 Business Hours |
| *3. Medium* | Partial service degradation. Affects a small group of users or a non-critical function (e.g., user can't access a shared folder, performance issue on a single PC). | 1 Hour | 2 Business Days |
| *4. Low* | Minor issue or inquiry affecting a single user. No significant business impact (e.g., software installation request, password reset). | 4 Business Hours | 5 Business Days |

#### *3.3. Service Response*

Service response is guaranteed during specified business hours. Critical (Severity 1) issues receive 24x7x365 support.

* *Support Channels:* Service Desk Portal, Dedicated Email (support@quantumit.com), and Phone (+91-80-XXXX-XXXX).
* *Business Hours:* 09:00 to 18:00 IST, Monday - Friday.
* *After-Hours Support:* Available only for Severity 1 and Severity 2 incidents via the dedicated phone line. Response for other severities will begin on the next business day.

#### *3.4. Exceptions and Limitations*

This SLA is subject to the following exceptions and special conditions:

* [Service Provider] is not liable for service degradation resulting from failures of third-party vendors, such as the Internet Service Provider (ISP). However, [Service Provider] will manage the vendor relationship to resolve the issue.
* Scheduled maintenance windows (notified to [Customer] 48 hours in advance) are excluded from availability calculations.
* [Service Provider] is not liable for service impact due to natural disasters, acts of terrorism, or other force majeure events.
* Issues resulting from user error, unauthorized changes made by [Customer] staff, or failure of [Customer]-owned hardware/software not under this agreement are excluded.

#### *3.5. Responses and Responsibilities*

*[Customer] responsibilities:*

* [Customer] shall designate a primary point of contact (POC) for all SLA-related matters.
* [Customer] will provide [Service Provider] with necessary physical and remote access to all covered systems.
* [Customer] shall inform [Service Provider] regarding changing business requirements or major environmental changes (e.g., new office location) at least 30 days in advance.
* [Customer] is responsible for the costs and licensing of all business-specific software applications.

*[Service Provider] responsibilities:*

* [Service Provider] will act as the primary support provider for all services identified in this agreement.
* [Service Provider] will conduct monthly performance review meetings and provide a detailed report covering all KPIs.
* [Service Provider] will inform [Customer] regarding scheduled and unscheduled service outages as promptly as possible.
* [Service Provider] will maintain up-to-date documentation of [Customer]'s IT infrastructure and processes.
* [Service Provider] will proactively monitor all covered systems to identify and resolve issues before they impact business operations.

*3.6.1. Service Availability*

Service coverage by the [Service Provider] as outlined in this agreement follows the schedule specified below:

* *On-site support:* Available from 09:00 to 18:00, Monday to Friday, upon request and subject to the terms in Appendix A.1.
